Meet Your Instructor

Hi, I’m Noah Wieder. I’ve been in the ATM business for over 30 years. Here’s my story. After graduating college, I packed up everything I owned in the custom van I built while in college. I drove from upstate New York blizzards to sunny San Diego, California. I had no job lined up, I was just tired of the snow and wanted sunshine and new opportunities, so I started hustling! In 1990 I saw the merchant processing business evolving and worked in that industry until I became the first employee of U.S. ATM. I was certified as one of the first Tidel ATM Technicians, then went on to found XtraCash ATM for National Bankcard Association, Inc. I went one to become one of the first five people asked to become part of the very first Triton ATM Advisory Committee.


I worked my way up the ranks and held quarterly business opportunity training seminars for 4 years and trained over 1000 ATM entrepreneurs. In 1996, I approached the West Coast Mcdonald's controller about placing ATMs in McD’s and I installed ATMs in 190 San Diego area McD’s. We performed various promotional tests and coined the term McMoney, which was approved by McD’s corporate back then. All in all, from 1995 - 2000, I installed over 5,000 ATMs nationwide! In 2000 I became V.P. of ATM Sales & Marketing for CIBC until 2002 when they decided to sell to eTrade. They wanted me to continue my role, but I knew I could make bank on my own so I cashed out, took a year off, and started ATMDepot.com. I took a slow growth approach and didn’t raise any money. I grew the business all from cash flow. My first residual check was barely enough to buy a cup of coffee. Now, we are debt free, manage over 3,000 ATMs, and count almost 2000 IADs as our clients who rely on us for education, training, sales, service, & support.
